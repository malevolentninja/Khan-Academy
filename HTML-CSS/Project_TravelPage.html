Travel to Paris

Bonjour, Have you been to paris?

Paris has some of the most famous attractions including:

  • Notre Dame
  • Le Louvre
  • The Eiffel Tower
  • Arc de Triomphe
  • Champs-Élysées

Paris isn't just about the attractions, it has the charm cafes, bistros and brasseries. Let us not forget about the wine and relaxed atmosphere created by Parisians.


Travel Suggestions

From my experience of Paris there is so much to do, it can't be done in simply a couple of days. However a couple of days is more than enough to gather a sample of what Paris has to offer.

There are always things to do :

  1. Visiting attractions
  2. Walking along the boulevards
  3. Sampling the culinary delights Paris has to offer.

Places to stay
